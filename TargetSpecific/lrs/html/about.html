CINEMAWARE

Cinemaware was originally founded in 1985, and since then has become a well-known brand of interactive entertainment software for next-generation game platforms and the Internet.

Cinemaware’s portfolio of hit game properties includes Robin Hood: Defender of the Crown®, Defender of the Crown, SDI™, The King of Chicago™, Sinbad and the Throne of the Falcon™, Rocket Ranger™, The Three Stooges®, Lords of the Rising Sun™, It Came from the Desert™, Wings™ and the TV Sports™ franchise of sports games. These titles still reside among numerous “Best Games of All Time” lists in the video game industry, have collectively sold millions of units across multiple platforms and are considered some of the most innovative and beloved games ever created in the history of video games.

Go to http://www.cinemaware.com for more information.
